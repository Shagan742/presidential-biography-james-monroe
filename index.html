James Monroe

Early Life

James Monroe was born on April 28, 1758 in Westmoreland County, Virginia. His parents were Spence Monroe, a great tobacco planter of Scottish descent, and Elizabeth Jones Monroe, who was of Welsh descent. Some of his siblings were James Spence Monroe, Andrew Monroe, and Joseph Monroe. His family owned 500-600 acres in Virginia and he was a decently respected rich kid with ample education.
